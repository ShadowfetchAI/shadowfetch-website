About

A fast, feed-driven newsroom with room to grow into something bigger.

ShadowFetch News is designed as a broad front page first: reliable sources, strong visual hierarchy, and a structure that can later absorb original roundups, opinion, interviews, and more.

Mission

Make a static site feel immediate.

The project uses prepared feed snapshots plus a fallback layer so GitHub Pages can still feel quick and current. It is intentionally broad, covering general headlines plus deeper sections for security, science, policy, business, and culture.
