How It Works

1. Start from your baseline

Set your starting percent once, enter the bill if you want the money math, and launch the verdict.

2. Swipe through service

Swipe right for strong service and left for weak service. Every card moves the tip up or down instantly.

3. Deliver the verdict

Arbiter shows the final percentage, split total, and evidence summary, then lets you share a sanitized public recap.

Privacy-First By Design

  • No accounts, no sign-in, and no vendor cloud sync.
  • No analytics SDKs, no ad tracking, and no cross-app profiling.
  • Restaurant names and short comments stay local unless you explicitly share them.
  • Shared verdicts are limited to the restaurant name, final percent, optional comment, generated chef graphic, and app name.
  • Bill totals, tip amounts, payment details, and server identity are not included in the social share message.
