Common Questions

Can I change the tip baseline?

Yes. You can set a default starting percent such as 18%, 20%, or 25%, and Arbiter remembers it on-device.

Can I edit the swipe questions?

Yes. You can edit, delete, disable, reorder, or add your own manifesto questions and keep your own permanent stack.

What does the share feature post?

The share output is limited to the restaurant name, final tip percentage, your short comment, a generated chef graphic, and the app name if enabled.

What does Arbiter not share?

It does not include bill totals, tip dollar amounts, payment details, or server identity in the social share message.

Does Arbiter require an account?

No. Arbiter works locally on your device and does not require sign-in.
