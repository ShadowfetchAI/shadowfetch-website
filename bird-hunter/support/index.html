Bird Hunter

Support

Bird Hunter is a local-first field guide and life-list app. If you need help with a screenshot for review, metadata clarification, or a real user workflow, start here.

Getting Started

Open the Identify tab, choose a species, and move into the detail screen. From there you can log a sighting, attach a photo from Camera or Photo Library, save notes, and optionally generate a trophy card through Apple’s system share sheet.

What Bird Hunter Stores

Bird Hunter stores favorites, compare-board selections, sighting logs, attached photos, thumbnails, and queued share cards locally on the device. Users can also opt into private iCloud sync for photo-backed records. The app does not require an account and does not depend on a developer-run cloud inbox.

Data Sources

Bird Hunter is powered by two open datasets:

  • BIRDBASE 2025.1
  • AVONET v7

Their names remain visible in the app and support materials because their open licenses still require attribution to accompany the data.
