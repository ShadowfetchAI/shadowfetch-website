Read the Bible one day at a time, fully offline.

Daily Word Journey gives you a personal 365-day reading plan, full Bible browsing, offline search, bookmarks, notes, highlights, and optional reminders in one calm native app.

What It Includes

  • personal 365-day Bible reading plan based on your chosen start date
  • King James Version and Douay-Rheims edition support
  • bookmarks, notes, and verse highlights
  • full offline Bible browsing and search
  • optional daily reminder notifications

Built as a Standalone Reader

Daily Word Journey is a standalone reading app. It does not require an account, does not include a donation flow, and does not depend on a hosted subscription service.
