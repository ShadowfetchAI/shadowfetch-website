Daily Word Journey

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Daily Word Journey is designed to work offline and to keep your reading experience simple and private.

Information We Collect

Daily Word Journey does not create user accounts and does not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, or payment card information through the app.

Information Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following information locally on your device:

  • your selected journey start date
  • your selected Bible edition
  • text size and theme preferences
  • reminder preferences
  • reading progress
  • bookmarks
  • notes and verse highlights

This information stays on your device unless you choose to back up your device through Apple services.

Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, the app requests permission to send local notifications on your device. These reminders are scheduled locally.

Third-Party Services

Daily Word Journey does not include third-party advertising SDKs or custom analytics SDKs.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. If it changes, the updated version will be posted at this page.
