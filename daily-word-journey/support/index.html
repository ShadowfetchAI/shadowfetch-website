Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app work offline?

Yes. After the Bible data is copied on first launch, the app reads fully offline.

How does the reading plan work?

You choose your own journey start date. The app maps each reading day to your personal 365-day journey.

Can I change the Bible edition?

Yes. You can choose between the King James Version and Douay-Rheims editions in Settings.
