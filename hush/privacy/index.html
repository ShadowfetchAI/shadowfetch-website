Hush Secret Text

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

Hush Secret Text is designed to work locally on your device. It does not run a message server, create user accounts, or store your messages in the cloud.

Information We Collect

Hush Secret Text does not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, contacts, or message content through the app.

Information Stored on Your Device

The app may temporarily store text you enter during your current session so you can encrypt or decrypt it locally on-device. Hush Secret Text does not operate a remote inbox or cloud message history.

Encryption

Hush Secret Text uses Apple CryptoKit to encrypt and decrypt text locally on your device using a shared secret phrase that you enter. Your shared secret is not uploaded to a server by the app.

Third-Party Services

Hush Secret Text does not include third-party advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or hosted messaging services.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. If it changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
