Frequently Asked Questions

Does Hush send messages for me?

No. Hush encrypts and decrypts text locally. You copy the encrypted text into another app such as Messages or Mail yourself.

Does Hush need an account?

No. There is no account creation, inbox, or server-side message delivery inside the app.

How do two people use it?

Both people agree on the same shared secret phrase. The sender encrypts text with that phrase, and the receiver enters the same phrase to decrypt it locally.

Does Hush store my messages in the cloud?

No. Hush is designed as a local encryption utility, not a hosted messaging service.
