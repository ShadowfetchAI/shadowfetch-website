How We Work

Built like something you would keep in the kitchen drawer.

The aim is simple: make a small tool, explain it honestly, and keep it useful enough that somebody reaches for it again.

Plain Talk

No puffed-up words when a simple sentence works.

Each app gets described by the job it does, the person it helps, and the everyday mess it tries to make smaller.

Good Manners

The apps should stay out of the way.

A good helper does its part, keeps the flow understandable, and lets people get back to the work, family, school, or road in front of them.
