Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

Renew Guard stores subscription records locally on the user’s device, does not require an account, and does not upload renewal data to a Shadowfetch backend.

What the app stores

Renew Guard stores subscription names, costs, billing cycles, dates, notes, optional links, and optional screenshot-derived fields locally on the device.

Photos and OCR

If the user imports a screenshot, the image is processed on device using Apple Vision to help fill subscription fields.

Calendar and notifications

Calendar access and local notifications are optional system features used only when the user chooses reminder-related actions.

Analytics and tracking

Renew Guard does not include analytics SDKs, ad SDKs, fingerprinting, or cross-app tracking.

Data sharing

Shadowfetch does not sell, rent, or share subscription data because the app does not collect it on a vendor backend.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the public support page for the Shadowfetch support page.
