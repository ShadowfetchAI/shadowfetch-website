Route Pay

Mileage tracking built for people who need clean numbers, not guesswork.

Route Pay helps commuters, merchandisers, delivery workers, and route-based teams log every leg of a workday, separate paid and unpaid miles, and export reports that are ready to send.

App flow

Built around route workers instead of generic expense forms.

  • Create a route day and log as many stops as the workday needs.
  • Mark each leg as paid or unpaid so the reimbursement math matches reality.
  • Use templates, saved locations, weekly defaults, and custom labels to cut repeat entry down.
  • Export local reports without creating an account or sending route logs to a Shadowfetch server.
