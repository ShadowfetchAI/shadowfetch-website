What It Does

Track the drive. Keep the record.

Route Pay was built as a dependable mileage workflow. It combines trip estimation, day-level review, saved history, and export so the user can understand whether a route is worth taking and still keep a clean record after the drive is over.

Core workflow

  • Estimate route value before leaving using distance and pay assumptions.
  • Log daily driving records and review them later by day.
  • Keep saved items and trip history available in one place.
  • Export records when the user needs to share, reconcile, or archive them.

Who it fits

Route Pay fits drivers, contractors, couriers, field workers, and anyone who needs a simple mileage-first record instead of a bloated fleet platform.
