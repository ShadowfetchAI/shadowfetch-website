Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Route Pay stores mileage workflow data on the device and does not require an account, analytics SDKs, or a Shadowfetch-operated cloud backend.

What the app stores

Route Pay stores route estimates, trip records, saved history, settings, and export-ready records locally on the user’s device.

Exports

Exports happen only when the user initiates them from inside the app. Route Pay does not automatically send records anywhere.

Analytics and tracking

Route Pay does not include advertising SDKs, cross-app tracking, fingerprinting, or user analytics in the current release.

Data sharing

Shadowfetch does not sell, rent, or share Route Pay records because the app does not collect them on a vendor server.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the Shadowfetch support page.
