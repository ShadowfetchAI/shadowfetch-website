Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Route Pay is designed to keep route and mileage reporting simple, local, and easy to control.

Information We Collect

Route Pay does not create user accounts and does not directly collect personal information such as your email address, phone number, or payment card information.

Information Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following information locally on your device:

  • saved route days and mileage logs
  • saved locations
  • saved route templates
  • custom labels and route defaults
  • theme, week-start, export, and interface preferences

This information stays on your device unless you choose to back up your device through Apple services.

Exports

CSV, image, and PDF reports generated by Route Pay are saved locally on your device in the app's files.

Analytics and Tracking

Route Pay does not use third-party advertising SDKs, does not use cross-app tracking, and does not include custom analytics collection.
