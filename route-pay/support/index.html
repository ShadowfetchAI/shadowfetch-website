Common Questions

Can I track multiple stops in one day?

Yes. Route Pay is built for single-stop or multi-stop workdays.

Can I exclude commute miles?

Yes. You can mark legs as unpaid or subtract an unpaid mileage adjustment.

Can I export reports?

Yes. Route Pay can save or share CSV, PDF, and summary outputs locally from the app.

Can I customize the workflow?

Yes. Route Pay supports custom labels, weekly defaults, week-start choice, mileage precision, export filename prefix, theme, and optional floating calculator visibility.

What if an export does not open?

Open the route day again, generate the export one more time, and choose Share or Save from the iOS sheet. If the problem continues, email support with the export type that failed.
