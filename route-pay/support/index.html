Support Topics

Common Route Pay questions.

What does Route Pay do?

Route Pay helps users keep route estimates, daily trip records, saved history, and export-ready summaries organized in one local-first workflow.

Does it require an account?

No. Route Pay does not require account creation, subscription login, or a vendor-run cloud backend.

How do exports work?

Exports happen only when the user explicitly chooses to share or save them from inside the app.

What should users include in a support request?

Device model, iOS version, the screen where the problem occurred, and whether the issue affects saved records or exports.
