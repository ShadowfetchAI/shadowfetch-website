Shift Swap Liaison

Fast team shift handoffs.

Shift Swap Liaison turns a messy chat thread into a simple handoff. Create the shift, share an app-only QR or link, let a teammate claim it in the app, and send the acceptance back to the requester and manager without running a backend.

Support Privacy Policy
App-only routing

Every invite is a Shift Swap Liaison QR code or link, so coworkers land in the claim screen instead of a generic web page.

Manager-ready follow-up

Once a teammate accepts, the app prepares the requester email and manager CC flow instead of asking users to rewrite the handoff manually.

Group status update

The accepter can post a claimed update back to the original chat so the rest of the team sees that the shift is tentatively covered.

Built for submission.

The current build is version 1.0 (1) with local-only defaults, no accounts, no tracking, and live support and privacy pages for App Store review.

How It Works

1. Create the swap

Enter your shift title, manager, time window, location, and any notes. The app also remembers your defaults for the next handoff.

2. Share it where the team already talks

Post the generated QR code or app-only link into iMessage, WhatsApp, Telegram, or the team thread you already use.

3. Close the loop

The teammate accepts inside the app, sends the requester and manager notice, and can post the claimed update back to the group so duplicate claims slow down.
