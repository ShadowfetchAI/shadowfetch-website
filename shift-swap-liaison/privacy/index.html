Shift Swap Liaison

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

Shift Swap Liaison is designed to coordinate simple shift handoffs without creating accounts, without running a team backend, and without tracking users.

Information We Collect

Shift Swap Liaison does not create user accounts and does not collect personal data on a vendor server.

Information Stored on Your Device

Shift Swap Liaison stores the following data locally on your device:

  • your saved name, email, and optional phone number
  • your default manager name and email
  • your default team or group label
  • recent shift requests you created
  • shift details you enter such as title, time, location, and notes
  • preference toggles such as manager CC, text preference, install hint, and claimed group update prompt

This information stays on your device unless you choose to back up your device through Apple services.

Camera Access

If you allow camera access, Shift Swap Liaison uses the camera only to scan app-only QR codes for shift requests and claimed status updates. The app does not upload or store camera captures.

Sharing and Communication

When you choose to send a shift invite, claim response, or claimed group update, Shift Swap Liaison uses Apple's system share sheet, Mail composer, or Messages composer.

Nothing is sent until the user confirms it. Managers do not need the app. Coworkers do need the app to open the app-only QR code or app-only link and respond inside the claim screen.

Analytics and Tracking

Shift Swap Liaison does not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking.
