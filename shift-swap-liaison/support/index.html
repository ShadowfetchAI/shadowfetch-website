Common Questions

Does the manager need the app?

No. The manager only needs the confirmation email the app prepares. Managers do not need Shift Swap Liaison installed.

Do coworkers need the app?

Yes. The QR code and link are app-only. A coworker needs Shift Swap Liaison to open the claim screen and accept or decline the shift.

Does the app send the email automatically?

No. Shift Swap Liaison opens Apple's Mail composer, Messages composer, or the system share sheet so the user confirms before anything is sent.

How does it reduce duplicate claims?

After a teammate accepts, the app can generate a claimed update for the original group chat. That gives the team a visible status signal even though the app does not run a backend lock.

Does it connect to scheduling software?

No. Shift Swap Liaison is intentionally simple and local-first. It does not integrate with Workday, ADP, or corporate scheduling systems.
